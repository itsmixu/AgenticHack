## Sorrow and Sacrifice

A newly widowed man, overcome with sorrow for his late wife, accepts the help of a Mystical Phantom promising to bring her back. He's desperate to reunite with his lover, but how far is he willing to go?

Game started at **Hack Club's Daydream 2025** in under **24h**, and was developed further for **Daydream Global, placing 12th overall 🥳**

_These are the Unity Project files. For our build, check out [this repo!](https://github.com/itsmixu/daydream25/tree/main)_
